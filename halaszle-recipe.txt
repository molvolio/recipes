Ingredients:

- 2kg fresh carp
- 2 onions
- 2bigspoons paprika powder
- 1spoon tomato sauce
- 1spoon white wine
- salt
- 3litres water

Directions:
1. clean fish & separate filets from head and body
2. finely chop onions
3. put fish, onions  and salt into large pot, fill up with water
4. bring to boil
5. after some fish fat is appearing on surfaces, add 1bigspoon paprika powder
6. add wine, tomato juice and continue to cook for 30min
7. add other bigspoon paprika powder before approx. 15min before end
8. salt n pepper to taste
9. never ever stir the pot ingredients, gently move the pot occasionally to prevent from burning down
